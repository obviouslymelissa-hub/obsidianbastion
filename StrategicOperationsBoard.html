⚡ Command Authority

The Strategic Operations Board serves as the Fleet's top command authority. It's where major decisions are made — mission approvals, star designations, special recognitions, and the overall direction for the ships operating under our banner. The Board presents as a council of senior officers, but its guidance ultimately reflects the leadership of the presiding General, who ensures every vessel — including the Obsidian Bastion — stays aligned with the Fleet's goals and standards.
